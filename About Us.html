About Explore Malaysia

Tourism has one of the biggest roles in representing the colors and the details of a country. Unique cultures and traditions, beautiful townscapes and eccentric wilderness, never fail to keep the tourism business alive and attractive. Unfortunately, it has become a common concern that the importance of our environment are constantly getting overlooked; landmarks slowly getting ruined and organic wildlife getting killed.

Here in Explore Malaysia, we believe that we can make a stand towards this adversity by implementing a better mindset to the explorers of the country. We aim to accomplish this by exercising a new and innovative way of tour-guiding sightseers. As we uncover the quirks and charms of Malaysia, our team consisting of well-educated, eloquent speaking and multilingual trained tourist guides will passionately educate you through the importance of sustaining the natural landscapes of Malaysia. Our various tour packages consist of a mixture of activities that will positively impact our environment, priced at affordable expenses. We will ensure that our services will satisfy you and escort you in exploring Malaysia!
