Sunway Lagoon


Sunway Lagoon ranks among Southeast Asia's biggest theme parks and boasts rides, water slides, a man-made beach, a wildlife park, and Malaysia's first surf simulator.
Sunway Lagoon is a Water Park that is located at besides Sunway Pyramid Shopping Complex.
It is one of the Kuala Lumpur's top family attractions that offers varities of activities that will suit kids of all ages.
It is very exciting place to hangout with groups.
It has many events that can take you throughout day.
